Sentinel Browser

Browse with full visibility into what websites are doing behind the scenes — trackers, fingerprinting, data collection, and more.

What Sentinel monitors for you

Privacy Scanner
Real-time detection of cookies, third-party requests, and data collection practices on every page you visit.
Tracker Detection
Identifies advertising networks, analytics services, social trackers, and cryptomining scripts using a database of 4,000+ known trackers.
Fingerprint Watch
Catches sites that probe canvas, WebGL, AudioContext, and battery APIs to build a unique profile of your device without your consent.
AI Analyzer
Powered by Claude or Gemini — get a plain-English explanation of exactly what a site collects, who it shares data with, and any high-risk jurisdiction connections.
Setup Required Enable AI Analysis with an Anthropic or Google Gemini API Key
1
Get an API key — visit console.anthropic.com, sign in or create a free account, and navigate to API Keys in the left sidebar.
2
Create a new key — click Create Key, give it a name (e.g. sentinel-browser), and copy the key that starts with sk-ant-…. Store it somewhere safe — it won't be shown again.
3
Add it to Sentinel — open the AI tab in the right panel, select your provider (Claude or Gemini), click ⚙, paste your key, and click Save. Keys are encrypted and stored locally — they never leave your machine.
4
Analyze any site — browse to a website, then click the AI tab in the right panel and hit Analyze This Site. Sentinel will send the collected privacy data to Claude and stream back a detailed plain-English report.
